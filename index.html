About

I'm Amogh Nyapathy, an Online Gameplay Programmer at EA Sports, where I develop and optimize multiplayer systems for live service games. I hold a Master of Science in Interactive Entertainment from the Florida Interactive Entertainment Academy.

At FIEA, I worked in collaborative, studio-like environments building games with multidisciplinary teams, which strengthened my ability to engineer elegant solutions in C++ while working closely with artists, designers, and producers. This foundation prepared me for the fast-paced, player-focused nature of AAA sports game development.
Outside of work, I enjoy playing piano and staying engaged with the eSports community, which keeps me connected to the gaming ecosystem and drives my passion for creating exceptional player experiences.
